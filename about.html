About AI Reckoning

AI Reckoning is an emergency response system for professionals facing the rapid transformation of the job market due to artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

To provide immediate, actionable guidance for professionals whose careers are threatened by AI advancement. We offer emergency transformation roadmaps, survival resources, and community support for rapid career pivoting.
